BEST OF UTTARAKAND

Day 1

RISHIKESH

In the morning drive to Rishikesh and upon arrival transfer to a hotel. You can unwind at the hotel, go for a walk to catch a glimpse of the scenic beauty or can enjoy boating . Return to the hotel for overnight stay.

Day 2

MUSSOORIE

After visiting the rishikeshi and visiting the mussoorie.Post breakfast, we will be taking you for a sightseeing tour of Mussoorie. We will be visiting the most famous tourist charms of the place. You will be free to explore the local markets and grab some memories to be cherished forever.

Day 3

AULI

After having the breakfast in the hotels and visiting the places in auli

Day 4

KAUSANI

After having the breakfast in the hotels and visiting the places in Kausani.Early in the morning after breakfast, we will leave for Kausani via Ranikhet. In Ranikhet, we will be visiting Chaubatia, Kalika and Upat. Spend the day in leisure, followed by dinner and overnight stay in a hotel at Kausani

Day 5

KEDHARNATH

In the morning after breakfast, we will head off for kedharnath. You can enjoy the picture perfect scenery of the place, followed by dinner and overnight stay in the hotel.

Day 6

UTTARKASHI

Drive back to Delhi after breakfast and desparture from Uttarakshi. On arrival, we will transfer you to airport or railway station for your journey back home
